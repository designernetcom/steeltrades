About Us

Welcome to Steel Trades

Steel Trades are stocksits & distributers of MS Fittings, Flanges, Valves, Gaskets and Industrial hardware was founded by Mr. Shrikant Maniyar in the year 1989. Steel Trades is one of the oldest firm that stocks and supplies all kinds of MS Fittings, Flanges, Valves, Gaskets and Industrial hardware with it’s office and warehouse located in he industrial belt of Bhosari MIDC, Pune.

All the MS material we stock are as per industrial standards and manufactured by renowned companies. Supplying high standard quality material at competitive prices is the primary aim of our organisation.Under the guidance of our founder ( Mr. Shrikant Maniyar ) who has more than 2 decades of experience in the field of MS material. We try our best to ensure that the requirement of our customers w.r.t different sizes ( Diameters & Thickness ) type ( ERW, Seamless etc. ) are delivered properly and in a timely manner.

Mission

To happily satisfy our customer’s requirement by supplying high standard quality material at competitive prices.

To emerge as a leading market player (one stop shop) in the iron and steel industry for our customers.

To have clear vision and strong ethical business policies to achieve spectacular growth.

To ensure growth and development of our employees.

Vision

To be the premier supplier of high-quality MS Fittings, Flanges, Valves, Gaskets, and Industrial hardware in Pune and beyond, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence, customer satisfaction, and industry-leading standards.

We aspire to be the go-to source for MS material, known for our reliability, integrity, and ability to meet diverse customer needs with precision and efficiency.

Core Values

We are dedicated to delivering products of the highest quality, meeting or exceeding industry standards, and ensuring customer satisfaction.

Our commitment to integrity means we conduct our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices, building trust with our customers, suppliers, and employees.

We believe in the power of teamwork, fostering a collaborative environment where every employee is valued and encouraged to contribute their best.
