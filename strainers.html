Strainers

Strainers

Steel Trades offers a versatile range of strainers meticulously crafted to remove solid particles from liquid or gas pipelines. These strainers are available in a variety of materials such as Mild Steel (MS), Stainless Steel, and Cast Steel, ensuring durability and corrosion resistance. With a size range from ½” to 24”, they are suitable for various pipe diameters and flow requirements. The strainers boast a pressure rating from Class 150 to Class 2500, making them suitable for a wide range of pressure conditions. Additionally, their end connections, including Flanged, Threaded, Socket Weld, and Butt Weld, ensure ease of installation and adaptability to different piping systems.

Applications :
  • Oil and gas industry
  • Petrochemical industry
  • Chemical processing
  • Water and wastewater treatment
  • Power generation
  • HVAC
Advantages :
  • Removes solid particles, protecting downstream equipment and processes
  • Suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications
  • Available in various materials and sizes to suit different applications
  • Versatile end connections for easy installation
Specifications :
Material : MS, SS & CS
Size Range : ½ ” to 24 ”
End Connections : Flanged, Threaded, Socket Weld, Butt Weld
